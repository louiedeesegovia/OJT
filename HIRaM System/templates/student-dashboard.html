💡 How to use the Borrower's Dashboard
Search & Filter
Use the search bar to find equipment by name, serial number, or condition. Click the category buttons to filter by type.
Borrow an Item
Click Borrow on any item card to fill out a borrow request form. Your request will go through an approval process before it's confirmed.
Use the Cart
Click the +Cart icon on a card to add it to your cart. Go to your cart, located at the top right next to the logout button to borrow multiple items at once using Borrow All.
Read the Calendar
Click Details on any item to see its borrowing schedule. Yellow means pending request, Green means approved/borrowed.
Check Your Requests
Go to My Requests from the landing page and enter your student ID to see the status of all your borrow requests.
Return on Time
Always return borrowed equipment by the agreed date. Late returns may affect your future borrowing privileges. Check your return date in My Requests.
